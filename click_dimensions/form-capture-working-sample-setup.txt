Settings > Click Dimensions > Form Captures

Create a Form Capture

Create Name
Associate with an existing Campaign
Visitor Entity (Lead or Contact)
Redirect on Error (Url on target site) e.g. http://runningclubrecords.com/?error=contact_form
Redirect on Success (Url on target site) e.g. http://runningclubrecords.com/?success=contact_form
Form Action (set by system)

Configure Form Capture Fields (sub-form) associated with this Form Capture

Hidden SMU has a label of dcc_smu and a form name of dcc_SMU
