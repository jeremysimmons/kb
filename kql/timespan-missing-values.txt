time chart show zero for missing/null data.

exceptions
| where client_Type != "Browser"
| where type == 'StackExchange.Redis.RedisConnectionException'
| make-series count() default=0 on timestamp in range(ago(8h), now(), 10m) by cloud_RoleInstance
| render timechart
